Why Prio

Our end goal is to enable possibility of direct contact between anyone on the planet, to enable close cooperation between those people, regardless of social status, and support charities at the same time.

How it Works

To make this happen, we are reaching out to well-known and respected people to give them a direct communication channel with other people who have a similar vision. This is to bring priority and attention to tomorrow’s greatest unknown ideas.

What the this?

Our application is the first which attempts to monetize the attention of VIPs, by connecting people of action through cutting edge technology, making whole proces easy to use on daily basis.
