Introducing Prio

Features For Investors

Charity You'll Love.

By using Prio you are contributing to charity just by responding to people as you always do with other apps, but it is more efficient to focus on priority. Effective personal communication was never so greatly incentivized.

Why Prio

To enable possibility of direct contact and cooperation between influencers, people of success and average Joe, regardless of distance, connections or social status.

How it Works

To make this happen, we are reaching out to well-known and respected people to give them a direct communication channel with other people, who want to support charities at the same time.

What is Prio

Our application aims to monetize the attention of VIPs, by connecting people of action with transparent and easy to use process.

Filter your messages by Priority

Clean and Organized

Personal communication was never so simple. You get a list of high quality messages sorted by the value of donation. You can support a charity you love, by responding to people who want to connect with you.

Learn More
You set the rules

People support a charity important to you

Donations are distributed according to the rules set by you. You can choose multiple charities or just one. You can even charge your an "attention fee", which you can use for other goals important for you.

Register as VIP

Every VIP user is able to set strict rules: charities (by name or category) you want to support, detail distribution of funds between those institutions, required minimum donation to contact you, whether specific conversations are marked as public or private.
